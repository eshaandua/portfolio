How I Think

The D.U.A. Framework

Every brief I write — for every brand on this page — runs through the same four-step thinking process I named after something that’s been there all along.

D
Diagnose
Find the gap nobody named

I map what the brand says, what the consumer hears, and what competitors ignore. Strategy lives in that triangle — and no one is looking for it.

U
Uncover
The one insight that changes everything

A strategy with 10 priorities has zero. I hunt the single consumer truth that makes the whole brief collapse into one obvious, unconfortable, brilliant move.

A
Articulate
Write it so it can’t be ignored

Data is my brief. Copy is my output. I write the headline, the tagline, the push notification, the billboard — until the idea has a voice the brand can’t unhear.

.
Activate
Strategy that ships on Monday

Slides that sit in decks are decoration. Every idea here comes with a channel plan, a campaign visual, and a brief that’s ready to hand to a team — not a consultant.
